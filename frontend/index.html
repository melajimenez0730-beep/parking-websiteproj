FAQ

Common questions

How long does the 3-minute hold last?

Once you select a spot on the map, it's locked exclusively for you for exactly 3 minutes. If you don't confirm in that window, the hold is released and the spot becomes available again. Just go back to the map and pick again.

Can I cancel my reservation?

Yes. From your reservation page, you can cancel any time before you check in. The spot is immediately released. If you've already checked in, please speak to on-site staff directly.

What happens if my reservation expires before I arrive?

Expired reservations are auto-released. Head back to the map, select a new spot, and re-book. If you have a Transaction ID from the expired booking and need assistance, show it to staff at the entrance.

Which floor should I choose?

Floor 1 is best if you're heading to the grocery area or want the quickest walk from the entrance. Floor 2 is the most popular all-rounder. Floor 3 is best for larger vehicles or when the lower floors are nearly full.

What vehicle types are accepted?

We accept cars and sedans, SUVs and vans, motorcycles, trucks and pickups, and vehicles with PWD (Persons with Disability) plates. Select your type accurately during booking so the system can assign the right spot.
